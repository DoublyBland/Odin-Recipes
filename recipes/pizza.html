Tasty Cheese Pizza

Pizza is a household favorite for many. It's an easy tasty treat!

Follow along with our simple instructions below to make your own pizza!

Ingredients

  • 2 Â½ cups warm water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 7 cups all-purpose flour
  • 6 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 1 tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Add the sauce
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Bake at 450 for 10 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
