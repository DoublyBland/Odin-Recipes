Simple Sandwich

Sandwiches are easy to pleasy

Follow along below to find yourself the owner of a tasy sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • slice lunch meat of your choice
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • Thinly sliced onion
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Sauce of choice

Steps

  1. Place one piece of bread on counter
  2. Add sauce
  3. Add meat, cheese, and veg
  4. Top with second slice of bread
  5. Enjoy!
